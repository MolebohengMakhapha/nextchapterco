About Next Chapter Co

Get to Know Us

Discover Our Story

Next Chapter Co is a specialized brand consulting firm dedicated to helping South African footballers transition from on-pitch performers to powerful personal brands. We guide upcoming, semi-pro, and professional players in building authentic profiles that attract sponsorships, grow social influence, secure endorsements, and create lasting legacies beyond the game.

Founded by Selebogo Ruth Lekalake, we understand the unique challenges and opportunities in South African football. Our approach combines strategic planning, content creation, social media mastery, and sponsorship positioning — all tailored to the local context and global aspirations of SA footballers. From pitch talent to profile power: we help you write your next chapter.

Football-Focused

Deep understanding of SA football culture, PSL realities, and what brands seek in athlete partners.

Proven Results-Driven

Strategies that deliver real growth: more followers, better engagement, and tangible off-field opportunities.

  • Selebogo Ruth Lekalake
